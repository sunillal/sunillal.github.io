Sunil Lal

 
 

Sunil Lal is currently a Senior Lecturer at the University of the South Pacific, Suva, Fiji. He is also a member of the Complex System Laboratory, University of the Ryukyus, Japan where he obtained his PhD under the guidance of Prof. Koji Yamada and Prof. Satoshi Endo. His research interest lies in the field of computational intelligence, which deals with problem solving using techniques inspired by nature. Of particular interest are robotics, evolutionary algorithms, machine learning, neural networks and cellular automata.
